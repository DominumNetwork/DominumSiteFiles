🔧 Step 1: Powerwash
  • Press Ctrl + Alt + Shift + R on the sign-in screen
  • Confirm the reset
  • OR press Esc + Refresh ↻, then press Power ⏻
🛠️ Step 2: Enter Recovery Mode
  • Press Ctrl + D on the recovery screen
  • Press Enter
👤 Step 3: Sign In (Initial)
  • Sign in normally
  • Immediately disconnect from your network
  • Press Refresh ↻ + Power ⏻ to perform an instant restart
🔐 Step 4: Sign In Again
  • Sign in as an existing user
🧩 Step 5: Extension Install
  • Navigate to: chrome://extensions
  • Reconnect to your network
  • Wait for the targeted extension to install automatically
🚫 Step 6: Final Disconnect & Re-login
  • Immediately disconnect from the network
  • Sign out
  • Sign in again
